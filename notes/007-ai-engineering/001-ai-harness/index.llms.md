# AI Harness

> **NOTE:**
>
> The harnesses covered in this part are mainly semi-autonomous and widely used as coding assistants or CLI tools.

There are various AI harnesses that I use, each with its own unique strengths.

The one that I think works the best out-of-the-box is Claude Code. It can be bloated, token-inefficient, and expensive, but this is the one I use at work and the company covers the subscription.

Then I also use Antigravity CLI (formerly Gemini CLI) to fix, format, and organize text like this Obsidian vault (strictly for drafts, not stuff I post to the public, which I try my best to make it my own words and writing style).

Finally, I also enjoy spending time tweaking and using Pi (pi-coding-agent) for hobby projects (along with Claude Code).
