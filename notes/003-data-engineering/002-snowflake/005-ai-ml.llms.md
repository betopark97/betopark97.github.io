# AI & ML

The AI & ML services from Snowflake has been undergoing a lot of changes. That’s why the notes for this section might also change frequently.

The architecture in a nutshell looks something like:

1.  Make a semantic view -\> this becomes a Cortex Analyst
2.  Make an agent, go to Configuration \> Tools \> Query structured data -\> Add semantic view

That’s pretty much it. But you also have to configure your agent with other stuff like Instructions, Skills, and MCP if you want more harness for your agent.
