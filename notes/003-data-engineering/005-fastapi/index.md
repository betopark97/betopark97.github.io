Yes, FastAPI is a backend. It feels weird to put FastAPI inside a data engineering section. However, these are notes to managing the backend for a data platform. The boundaries with an actual full blown app will be quite blurry, but it is a skill that I want to practice and put under my belt because Tableau and Streamlit gets you so far.

The frontend for this backend has a few choices. I used to just do HTML, CSS, and vanilla Javascript because of the advancements on AI making this easier to do. However, it bloats really fast and it's hard to wrap your head around most of the features when the app grows. That's why I think from now on I will choose to go with NiceGUI.
