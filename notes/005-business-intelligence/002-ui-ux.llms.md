# UI / UX

The importance in dashboards is to convey what the underlying data means effectively to the users, but for the users to engage with your dashboard, the ui/ux is very important. I’ve come to realize that if it’s difficult to find filters, to know (get feedback) if something is working as expected or not, and if the layout is not straightforward, people do not engage with your dashboard and just want the raw data to investigate by themselves on a spreadsheet like Excel.

The tools that I’ve come to use for my UI/UX and dashboarding is streamlit and streamlit-shadcn-ui.
