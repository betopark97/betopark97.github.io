# Visualization

Choosing the appropriate visualization for data is essential.

It depends a lot on what the stakeholder wants to see. Communication is key. Instead of make filters and dimensions everywhere, ask what they want to see and try to only make visualizations that they want to see. Remember that not every stakeholder has high data literacy, make it simple and straightforward. Something that will help them quickly understand their data and make decisions.
