title: Intro
---
Business intelligence is not about making flashy and cool graphs. It's about delivering data insights for data driven decisions.

There are various tools to consider when making dashboards. The one that I like the most because of its simplicity is Streamlit. It comes with so many downsides when compared with actual backend + frontend stacks in relation to performance at large scale. However, all-in-all it's a great tool to make dashboards for business intelligence.
