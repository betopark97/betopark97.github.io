# Definition

Supply Chain Management consists of different components:

1.  Planning
    - S&OP
    - Demand Plan
    - Supply Plan
    - Inventory
2.  Sourcing
    - Procurement
    - SRM
    - Supplier Negotiation
3.  Manufacturing
    - Production Scheduling
    - Capacity Planning
4.  Logistics
    - Warehousing
    - Transport
    - Last-Mile
