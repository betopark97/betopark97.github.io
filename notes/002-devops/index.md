title: DevOps
icon: infinity
description: Automation and integration of development and operations to deliver faster and more reliably.
---
This section will have technical notes covering the overall workflow of the DevOps lifecycle starting with local development setup and version control to CI/CD and automated deployment.

It documents the processes, infrastructure, and automation required to deliver software reliably and efficiently.

>[!NOTE] 
> Although the documentation is biased for macOS, DevOps requires Linux foundations as the development environment may be macOS, but the production environment will most certainly be Linux.
